Upcoming and Past Events

Through impactful health initiatives, the foundation continues to champion wellness by organizing screenings, awareness campaigns, and community engagements. These efforts aim to promote early detection, prevention, and improved healthcare access. Below are details of our upcoming and past events:

#1. World Kidney Day 2025 Free Health Screening

The Firm Health Ghana Foundation, in collaboration with Government Hospital, Bogoso and GhanaWeb, is organizing a free health screening to commemorate World Kidney Day 2025. This initiative aims to promote early detection, prevention, and education on kidney health, ensuring that individuals receive essential medical check-ups and guidance on maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Objective: To address critical health concerns related to kidney disease, diabetes, and hypertension by providing free health screenings, raising awareness about early detection, and promoting preventive healthcare measures.
Implementation: Organizing a free health screening event in partnership with medical experts from Bogoso Government Hospital and Tarkwa Community Hospital. The program will offer Blood Urea and Creatinine Tests, Blood Glucose Tests, and Blood Pressure Checks, alongside community engagement activities to educate residents on kidney health.
Impact - The initiative will help detect kidney-related issues early, reducing the risk of severe complications and lessening the financial burden of costly treatments such as dialysis or transplants. By providing free medical check-ups, the program ensures that underserved communities gain access to essential healthcare services, ultimately improving overall public health.
