Dr. Sylvester Akpah

Sylvester Akpah has over six (6) yearsâ€™ experience in NGO management. Presently, as the Chief Executive Officer of Firm Health Ghana Foundation, Sylvester leads a team of 15 medical and administrative staff to advocate for the risk factors of kidney health. At the moment, the foundation has implemented four (4) major interventions in the Tarkwa Nsuaem Municipality, namely: a weekly public health education program on Dynamite FM (88.9), quarterly free kidney screening, monthly 5K health walks with aerobics sessions, and a diabetes and hypertension clinic.
Sylvester is a Lecturer in the Computer Science and Engineering Department of the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), specializing in network infrastructure deployment, wireless security, software engineering, and web technologies. He holds an MSc and BSc in Information Technology from the Ghana Technology University College (Ghana) and Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (Ghana), respectively. Sylvester also volunteers as the Western Regional Coordinator for Operation Smile Ghana, an NGO that provides free medical surgeries to persons with facial deformities, specifically cleft lip/palate. He is a 2018 Mandela Washington Fellow of Drexel University (Philadelphia). Sylvester enjoys watching football and exercising.
